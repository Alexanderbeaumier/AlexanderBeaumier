About Me

I’m Alexander Beaumier, a Cloud Engineer with extensive experience in designing, deploying, and managing secure AWS cloud infrastructures. My expertise lies in automating deployments, provisioning cloud resources, and optimizing system performance using industry-leading technologies like AWS, Docker, and Terraform. I am passionate about implementing Infrastructure as Code (IaC) to build scalable, cost-effective cloud solutions.

My professional journey began with a Bachelor’s degree in Urban Planning, where I developed a strong analytical and problem-solving foundation. Transitioning into cloud engineering, I honed my skills in managing cloud environments, optimizing databases such as Amazon RDS and DynamoDB, and ensuring the highest standards of security through IAM policies and compliance protocols. I have since been focused on DevOps automation, cloud security, and high-performance infrastructure design.

Currently, I’m pursuing a Master of Science in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance, furthering my expertise in cloud security, threat mitigation, and regulatory compliance. Through advanced certifications, such as AWS Cloud Practitioner and ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity (in progress), I’m continuously expanding my knowledge in cutting-edge technologies like AI services (AWS Textract, SageMaker) and cloud architecture.
